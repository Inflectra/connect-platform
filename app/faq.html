How does the InflectraCon virtual conference work?

tl;dr Each day has different videos - available on demand anytime. Every speaker and trainer for that day will be online part of the day to chat with you and answer any questions.

All sessions at InflectraCon are available on-demand: watch what you want, when you want. Feel free to pause, rewind, speed it up, or slow things down. Each day different sessions will be available - you have 24 hours to watch that day’s sessions, then the next day starts. Days run from midnight to midnight East Coast US time.

Each day has different videos so that we can make sure the speakers and trainers for all that day’s sessions will be available on our live community to chat, discuss and answer all your questions. Speakers and trainers are only live online at certain times (they need to sleep and eat and things like that)

What talks or sessions can I watch today?

All the sessions for each day are listed on the home page / program page, broken down by track. When you first go to the page, you will see today’s sessions, but you can look at other days too.

To check out a track, go to that track’s page and look through the playlist of videos - these are all the videos you can watch today.

How do I watch sessions?

Head to the page of the track you want to watch. You will see a big video playlist in the middle of the page with today’s videos.

Why are some videos behind a padlock?

You will need a password to watch the videos on the Workshop and Advanced Topics track. Full Access ticket holders will have an email with their password in it. If you’re not a Full Access ticket holder head over here to upgrade your ticket!

I am a Full Access ticket holder but can’t find my password

If you can’t find the email with your password please go to the conference #front-desk and they can help you.

I want to ask the speaker a question

Each speaker or trainer is available on our community platform (Discord). The program and each track page shows when each speaker is live on their channel. To get to the right place on Discord click the name of the speaker or follow the links on the track page.

Can I talk to other attendees?

Of course! You can chat to anyone on our Discord. The #hallway room (channel as Discord calls it) is a great place to hang out. If you have access to the Workshop or Advanced Topic channels you can ask questions or share thoughts with others about those sessions (as well as with the trainers).

How do I use the chat sidebar?

The chat sidebar connects to Discord, so first make sure you have set up your Discord account. Login to Discord in the same browser. Then click the "Login" button at the bottom of the chat sidebar. Then make sure to click DISCORD LOGIN. This will open a new tab asking you to allow Titan Embeds to access your Discord. Allow this. You are now all set.

I need some help with the conference

Please go to the conference #front-desk and ask one of the InflectraCon team for help.

Will the conference sessions’ content be made available after the conference?

Yes! If you have a Full Access ticket to InflectraCon™ 2020 Virtual, once the conference has ended you will be able to watch all training sessions on this website until the end of October 2020. A few handpicked talks will also be made public on our YouTube channel.

What does InflectraCon 2020 Free Pass give me access to?

With a Free Pass, you get on-demand access to all Keynotes and all sessions on the Thought Leadership, and Inflectra IRL tracks.

What time zone is InflectraCon running in?

InflectraCon™ 2020 is an on-demand, virtual experience, with the program kicking off at 12:00 AM on September 29 EDT/GMT-4. All times on this site are shown in local times for you.
